About

I am a Research Scientist at the MIT Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics, collaborating with Prof. Jonathan How and Prof. Nicholas Roy. My research lies at the intersection of robotics, deep learning, and control theory. I received the PhD (2020), SM (2017), and SB (2015) degrees from MIT in Mechanical Engineering. My PhD work was advised by Prof. Jonathan How, Prof. John Leonard, and Prof. Alberto Rodriguez.
